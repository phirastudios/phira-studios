Why Phira

Why our photography is different.

Traditional product photography is slow, expensive, and inconsistent at scale. AI flips the equation — but only when paired with brand discipline and craft. That's where Phira comes in.

01

Consistency at scale.

Whether you need 30 images or 300, every single one matches your brand exactly — same lighting, same mood, same aesthetic. Impossible with traditional shoots.

02

10x faster turnaround.

No location bookings, no models, no waiting for weather. From brief to final delivery in 5 to 7 days — even for hundreds of images.

03

Fraction of the cost.

Studio shoots run $200–500 per image. Our packages start at $50 per image with bulk options going lower — same quality, no compromise.

04

Endless variations.

Need the same product on white, in a studio, on a beach, in winter light? AI gives you infinite settings without reshooting. Perfect for seasonal campaigns.

05

Print-ready quality.

Every image is high-resolution, optimized for both web and print. Use them everywhere — site, Instagram, catalogs, billboards.

06

Brand-aligned, always.

We start by building a custom style guide for your brand. Every image flows from that foundation — never random, always recognizable.

Our Process

How we work, step by step.

A simple, polished process — from brief to final images in 5 to 7 days.

01

Brand Brief

Share your brand, mood references, sample products. Free 30-min call to align on aesthetic and goals.

02

Style Guide

03
